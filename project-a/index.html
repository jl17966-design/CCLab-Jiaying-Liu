Appearance:

A raindrop-like creature. Its head features two observant black eyes that track movement with precision. It typically glows with a white hue, while approaching, with a calm, cyan-blue hue. It will also change color when touched and disappear in the surrondings when continuously touched.

Key Characteristics:

  • Like a cat, curious about new things but also afriad of them
  • Can detect people’s approaching
  • Change in speed and color when touched
  • Can blend into the environment in extremely nervous status
